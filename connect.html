Connect

Over 500 UCLA students staff the Daily Bruin, serving thousands in the campus and Westwood community every single day. Here are ways we hope you’ll stay connected with the campus and your fellow alumni!

  • You can find Daily Bruin alumni groups on Facebook and LinkedIn. These are actively managed by The Bruin’s alumni director.

  • We anticipate our first annual reunion on campus in fall 2018. Stay tuned for future events and updates. For now, please reach out directly to the alumni director if you want to visit the office or have any questions. Our doors in Kerckhoff Hall are always open.

  • To stay up to date on all UCLA happenings, please bookmark dailybruin.com, subscribe to our daily newsletter and follow the newspaper across its social media sites – Twitter, Facebook and Instagram.

  • For fundraising specifics, please visit the Give section of the site.

  • Have ideas or questions? Please visit the Contact section to get in touch.
