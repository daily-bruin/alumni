Connect

Over 500 UCLA students staff the Daily Bruin, serving thousands in the campus and Westwood community every single day. Stay connected through alumni outreach efforts, along with the editorial work of the staff.

You can find Daily Bruin alumni groups on Facebook and LinkedIn. These are actively managed by The Bruin’s alumni director.

To stay up to date on all UCLA happenings, please bookmark dailybruin.com, subscribe to our daily newsletter and follow the newspaper across its social media sites – Twitter, Facebook and Instagram.

Stay tuned for future events the network hosts. For now, please reach out directly to the alumni director if you want to visit the office or have any questions. Our doors in Kerckhoff Hall are always open.

For fundraising specifics, please visit the “Give” section of the site.
