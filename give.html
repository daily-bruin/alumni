Give

Thank you for supporting the Daily Bruin! Your donation allows us to provide vital resources to young journalists at UCLA, enriching the community through valuable reporting. Any and all support ultimately promotes the free press, helping us train students to be discerning, thoughtful and relentless in their reporting.

Support will go directly into equipment, travel, training and scholarships – tangible things that make a huge difference in the Daily Bruin experiences of every staffer. You can always get in touch with the Daily Bruin alumni director to discuss details. Visit the “Contact” page for more information.

Here is a breakdown of the ways to give –

UCLA Student Media

The Daily Bruin is part of UCLA Student Media, which is overseen by the UCLA Communications Board. The Board is the independent publisher, manager and operator of the media organizations.

All donations to the Daily Bruin or its sister media organizations are tax-deductible since UCLA Student Media is an independent, nonprofit organization with 501(c)(3) status.

For your records, the federal taxpayer ID number for gifts to UCLA Student Media or any of its specific publications is 95-1777979.

Please visit this website to give directly to the Daily Bruin.

UCLA

Because UCLA Student Media operates within Associated Students UCLA – a student-controlled, non-profit organization – donations to the university can also be earmarked for the Daily Bruin’s operational costs.

Daily Bruin Alumni Association

We are working to establish a future fund that directly supports the alumni association.

Other ways to help

There is a wealth of journalistic knowledge among Daily Bruin alumni. Please reach out to the DB alumni director if you’re interested in getting involved through workshops, critiques or other valuable journalistic services. We always appreciate such in-kind donations.
